# Title Idol Style Guide

Use this guide when regenerating the title idols so they look like one series.

## Common Style

- Bright sunlit dance rehearsal studio with mirrors on the left, large windows on the right, warm wooden floor, and soft particles of light.
- Energetic Japanese anime idol-game title illustration, clean fine line art, luminous eyes, soft cel shading, crisp cheerful facial expression.
- White sporty idol lesson outfit with navy, blue/lavender/pink accent colors, small star or ribbon details, wristbands, short layered skirt, and sneakers.
- Landscape 16:9 composition, one female idol only, dynamic dance lesson pose, title-screen readability.
- Avoid text, logos, watermarks, extra people, distorted hands, extra fingers, extra limbs, duplicate faces, dark lighting, and heavy oil-paint style.

## Characters

- 白瀬ルナ / Luna Shirase: silver-haired slender pure idol, blue eyes, pale blue ribbon, calm but lively.
- 小羽ひなた / Hinata Kohane: energetic younger-sister idol, brown ponytail, pink ribbon, bright smile.
- 西園寺レイカ / Reika Saionji: refined ojou-sama idol, blonde hair, violet eyes, lavender ribbon and rose/star accent.
